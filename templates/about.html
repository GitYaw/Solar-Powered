About Solar Energy Prediction

This website uses a machine learning model to predict the amount of power generated by solar panels based on various weather conditions. The model takes into account factors such as cloud cover, dew point, humidity, precipitation, solar energy, solar radiation, sun elevation, temperature, and UV index to provide an accurate prediction.

Our goal is to help users understand the potential solar energy output for a given set of weather conditions, enabling better planning and optimization of solar power systems.
